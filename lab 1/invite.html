WEDDING INVITATION LETTER

Julia French, daughter of Mr. Adam French and the late Iris French, and
Austin Mahoney, son of Mr. Camden and Elizabeth Mahoney,

request the honor of your presence at their wedding on the fifth of May, two thousand seventeen at one o'clock in the afternoon The Reagan Library Simi Valley, California

Dinner & dancing to follow Black tie required

  • Location: The Reagan Library Simi Valley, California
  • Time: 13:00 PM
